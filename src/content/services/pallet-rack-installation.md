## Professional Pallet Rack Installation — Illinois, Midwest & Nationwide

Redline Installers LLC has been installing pallet rack systems for warehouses, distribution centers, and manufacturing facilities for over 34 years. We handle everything from single-aisle repairs to complete 200,000 sq ft warehouse builds.

### What We Install

- **Selective pallet racking** — the most common system; full front access, ideal for high-SKU operations
- **Drive-in / drive-through racking** — high-density storage for low-SKU, high-volume environments
- **Push-back racking** — LIFO storage for 2–6-deep pallet lanes
- **Pallet flow racking** — gravity-fed FIFO for high-turnover perishables and date-sensitive product
- **Cantilever racking** — long, bulky material storage (pipe, lumber, structural steel)
- **Double-deep racking** — doubles density vs. selective with minimal forklift modification

### Our Installation Process

1. **Site assessment** — we measure your floor, assess columns, document existing infrastructure, review fire suppression and sprinkler clearances
2. **Layout design** — we produce a dimensioned floor plan optimized for your forklift, product dimensions, and throughput goals
3. **Permit coordination** — we handle engineering stamps and permit applications where required
4. **Staged delivery** — we coordinate rack delivery to minimize staging footprint and keep aisles clear
5. **Installation** — OSHA-compliant crew, anchored to spec, level and plumb to manufacturer tolerance
6. **Inspection & sign-off** — final walk-through, torque verification, column protector placement, and safety inspection report

### Industries We Serve

Pallet rack installations for warehousing, distribution centers, manufacturing, logistics, cold storage, food distribution, and industrial storage throughout Illinois and the Midwest.
