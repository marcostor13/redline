## Heavy-Duty Industrial Storage Installation

Standard warehouse rack systems are not designed for industrial material storage. Bar stock, structural steel, pipe, lumber, coiled wire, drums, and heavy fabricated components require rack systems engineered for the actual load, material length, and handling method — not a generic pallet rack specification applied to an industrial environment.

Redline Installers installs heavy-duty storage systems for industrial product distributors, steel service centers, fabricators, and wholesale industrial suppliers throughout Illinois and the Midwest. We size every system to your actual material weight, center-of-gravity, and handling equipment.

### Industrial Storage Systems We Install

**Cantilever rack** is the correct system for long and irregularly shaped material. Single-sided and double-sided cantilever columns with adjustable arms provide unobstructed full-length access without the vertical columns that interrupt selective pallet rack bays. We install light, medium, and heavy-duty cantilever systems for applications ranging from lumber and conduit to structural steel beams and fabricated assemblies.

**Structural pallet rack** — bolted hot-rolled construction rather than roll-formed sheet steel — handles the high point loads common in industrial distribution environments. Dense materials including castings, stampings, bar stock in baskets, and heavy fabricated parts often exceed the safe capacity of standard roll-formed rack. We specify and install structural rack where the material weight and load concentration require it.

**Drum and barrel storage systems** — vertical drum storage racks, horizontal drum cradles, and IBC tote storage frames — for chemical distributors, lubricant suppliers, and industrial fluid operations. We install containment-compliant configurations where regulatory requirements apply.

**Coil storage cradles** for steel and wire product distributors. We size cradle pitch and coil capacity to your coil diameter range and weight and coordinate installation with your overhead crane hook path so every cradle position is accessible without crane obstruction.

**Mezzanine platforms** for small parts and fastener storage above a heavy materials floor — creating a second level for high-SKU, low-weight inventory without competing with the heavy-duty floor-level systems below.

### Industrial Anchoring Standards

Industrial storage facilities require higher anchor pull-out ratings than standard warehouses because the rack loads and handling equipment impact forces are greater. Our crews specify and install the correct anchor type and pattern for your slab thickness, concrete compressive strength, and aggregate condition. Every anchor is torqued to specification with a calibrated wrench, and the torque log is included in the post-installation inspection report for your facility records and insurance carrier.
